About Go European

Go European is a free browser extension designed to help you discover European alternatives to popular non-European websites and services. Our goal is to promote digital sovereignty and support European tech companies.

The extension shows you relevant alternatives based on your location and preferences, making it easy to find and use services that align with European values and regulations.

Review & Use

Using this extension already shows plenty of support! Here's how you can help even more:

  • Leave a review on the extension store - this helps others discover Go European
  • Share the extension with friends and colleagues who might be interested in European alternatives
  • Use it regularly to discover European alternatives to non-European sites and services
  • Send us feedback about your experience with the extension and the alternatives you've discovered

Your feedback helps us improve and reach more users!
